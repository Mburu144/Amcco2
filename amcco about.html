OUR HISTORY

AMCCO Properties, was Established in July 2020, AMCCO Properties stands as a beacon in Kenya's real estate
sector. Our core vision and goal revolve around providing AFFORDABLE PLOTS FOR SALE , tailored for investors
eager to initiate immediate home construction.

The dynamic real estate landscape in Kenya reveals a clear gap, notably in the Property and Housing Market.
There is a robust demand for Residential properties necessitating prompt settlement, yet the supply side is
constrained, coupled with elevated land prices.

AMCCO Properties, as a market influencer, conducts thorough research within the Nairobi Metropolis,
concentrating on key areas like KIKUYU, THIGIO, GIKAMBURA, and KAMANGU. Our objective is to identify and
present Affordable Plots for Sale, presenting you with the prospect of owning a home within the city.

At AMCCO Properties, we are committed to bridging the gap by delivering genuinely affordable plots
strategically situated in high-demand areas. This aligns with our dedication to meeting the rising demand for
immediate settlement in Nairobi, Kenya. Discover the unparalleled offerings of AMCCO Properties, where
affordable plots converge with your dreams of a perfect home.
